Fatimah's Five Facts: On My Block

Fact #1

On My Block is a coming-of-age television series, created by Lauren Iungerich, Eddie Gonzalez, and Jeremy Haft. It was released on Netflix on March 16, 2018.

Fact #2

Consisting of young black and Latino actors in what is many of their first major roles. The first season currently holds a 93% approval rating on Rotten Tomatoes.

Fact #3

Jason Genao(who plays Ruby)'s favorite poem is “I’m Nobody! Who Are You?” by Emily Dickinson.
